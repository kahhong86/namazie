The Personal Data Protection Act (“PDPA”)

The Personal Data Protection Act (“PDPA”)

The PDPA came into force on 1 July 2014. The regulations applies to the collection of personal data of individuals obtain and used for marketing services or other activities. Under the PDPA, all organisations must comply with nine obligations, namely, consent, purpose, notification, access and correction, accuracy, protection, retention limitation, transfer limitation and openness obligations. We advise clients on issues of compliance with the PDPA and draft privacy policy statements for clients.
